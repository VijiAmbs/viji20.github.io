It has been observed that travellers are looking for a service that provides incredible,reassuring Covid related travel information. TravelSafe was designed to encourage them to make informed decisions while planning their next trip in a post pandemic world. Worked independently as a UX Researcher and Designer in this project.

THE PROCESS

USER INTERVIEW

Conducted an exploratory primary qualitative research by interviewing 5 users in a one-on-one interview format within a period of 1 week. The questions were structured, open-ended and aimed at understanding the mindset, values and behaviours of travelers in a post pandemic world.

DEFINITION & SYNTHESIS

Synthesized data with the help of an Affinity diagram which was condensed into an Empathy map that helped formulate the user persona. This helped simplify and identify the prolem most travelers face today - to stay updated with travel guidelines on Covid-19.
